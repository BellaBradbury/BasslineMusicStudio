Bass Line Music Studio was formed by Bella and Zack Bradbury as a way to give our music students the opportunity to not just learn independently but also through group performance. We know firsthand that music is one of the best tools for self-expression and growth and we love getting to offer a safe space where community and collaboration are prioritized!

We offer one-on-one lessons at our Toledo-based studio to students of any age or skill level in Voice, Piano, Drums, Guitar, and Bass. In addition to Private Lessons, we also run student bands that meet weekly to work towards End-of-Season Concerts where they are able to showcase their passion for playing to their family and friends.
