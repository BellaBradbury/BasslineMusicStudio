MEET THE TEAM

BELLA BRADBURY

Owner/Instructor

With over two decades of training and experience in the arts, Bella is excited to be able to share her knowledge! She began performing onstage at the young age of three and has since gone on to perform in over 100 productions spanning regional playhouses, indie film, and community projects.

When not teaching, she can be found around the community performing with The Crosswell Opera House, The Village Players, and other local companies. Favorite roles include Winnifred (Once Upon A Mattress), Annabel Glick (Lucky Stiff), and Greenpeace Girl (The Guy Who Didn't Like Musicals). Though Bella does not compete anymore, she has been honored to accept numerous awards over the years including: American Guild of Music 2016 NAIC Regional Broadway Champion and the 2015 OCTA JR Excellence of Acting Award.

ZACK BRADBURY

Owner/Instructor

Zack's love for the arts began because of and was fueled by his amazing High School Choir Teacher. Over the years he discovered a passion for the keys, drums, and bass in addition to vocal performance. He is very grateful to now be able to continue the cycle and teach future musicians!

With everything he does, Zack infuseses a level of passion that is hard to match. Outside of teaching, he enjoys virtual and tabletop gaming, studying local history, and perfecting new recipies.

EVELYN KOOP

Social Media Manager/Admin

Evelyn began her journey with Bass Line as a vocal student of Bella's and is now excited to be part of the team working to give other students more opportunities to perform!

When not actively pursuring her degree at Bowling Green State University, she enjoys spending time with her cats and scrolling social media- making her the perfect fit to be at the helm of Bass Line's online accounts.
