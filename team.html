MEET THE TEAM

BELLA BRADBURY

Owner/Instructor

With over two decades of training and experience in the arts, Bella is excited to be able to share her knowledge! She began performing onstage at the young age of three and has since gone on to perform in over 100 productions spanning regional playhouses, indie film, and community projects.

When not teaching, she can be found around the community performing with The Crosswell Opera House, The Village Players, and other local companies. Favorite roles include Winnifred (Once Upon A Mattress), Annabel Glick (Lucky Stiff), and Greenpeace Girl (The Guy Who Didn't Like Musicals).
